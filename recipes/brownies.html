Best Brownies

Description

Brownies are a chocolately classic. This simple yet delicious dish will have both kids and adults rushing to the kitchen

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • Semi-sweet chocolate
  • Cocoa-powder
  • Dash of salt
  • Touch of Love

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Melt butter and chocolate together
  3. Add egg and mix to combine
  4. Add dry ingredients and mix
  5. Cook for 40 minutes!
  6. Enjoy!!!!
