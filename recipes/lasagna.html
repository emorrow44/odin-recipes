Lasagna

Description

This classic italian casserole will wow you! It's so yummy yummy

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Tomatoes!
  • Ricotta cheese
  • lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Cook the meat with the onion and garlic
  3. Add tomatoes to cooking meat to make sauce
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, and ricotta in casserole dish, repeating until full
  5. Cook for 1 hour!
